Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 My company blocks the Microsoft AppSource Store. How can I install LegalLint? +

In many larger enterprises, the store is blocked for security reasons. Your IT department needs to install it for you via the SharePoint app catalog or the Microsoft 365 admin center. We can work together with your IT to ensure seamless integration. Don't hesitate to contact us for assistance.

📶 Can I use LegalLint without an internet connection? +

Previously, Word add-ins were executables installed directly on your machine. Microsoft now prefers to host the add-ins as web apps to ensure cross-compatibility across different operating systems (Windows, Mac, and Web). Therefore, you need an internet connection to start and authorize LegalLint. However, once you are in your Word document and do not close it, LegalLint can be used locally, as Word caches the code for 30 days and all processing happens on your machine.

🌍 Where is LegalLint hosted? +

Our add-in is hosted in Frankfurt, Germany. We do not store any logs. Additionally, when you start using our add-in, it gets cached on your local computer, and therefore, your document is processed locally, except for the standard terms checker, which requires a web call to our server because our AI is hosted there.

📄 Are my documents processed in the cloud? +

If you use LegalLint locally and do not use the standard terms checker, your documents are not moved out of your local computer. This ensures compliance with any data security regulations.

📝 Can LegalLint be used for non-legal documents? +

Absolutely! Although our main focus is on legal professionals, many of our features are also applicable in other areas, such as proofreading theses. Ideally, you can use it in combination with PerfectIt and Grammarly.
